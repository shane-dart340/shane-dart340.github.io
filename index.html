Broccoli and Bowties

This is what you need to make Broccoli and Bowties:

  • 12 oz. pasta
  • 1 lb. frozen broccoli
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 3 Tbsp grated Parmesan
  • Salt to taste
  • Freshly cracked pepper to taste
  • 1 pinch crushed red pepper

This is how you make it...

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil, then add the pasta.
  2. Boil until the pasta is al dente (7-10 minutes).
  3. Add the frozen broccoli florets to the boiling pasta water, turn off the heat, and let sit for 1-2 minutes
  4. Drain the pasta and broccoli in a colander.
  5. Transfer the pasta and broccoli back to the pot (with the heat off) or to a bowl and add the butter.
  6. Toss until the butter has melted and coated everything.
  7. Add the Parmesan, salt, freshly cracked pepper, and red pepper flakes, then toss to coat again.
  8. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed. Serve immediately.
